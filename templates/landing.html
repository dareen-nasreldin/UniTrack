Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

No recurring habits tracker, no project management overhead, no team collaboration. Just tasks, courses, and deadlines.

Quick capture

Add a task with just a title. No course, no date, no description required. Dump it now, sort it later.

Organized by course

Dashboard groups tasks by course: ECE241, MAT291, Internships. One view for your whole semester.

Deadline calendar

A monthly calendar shows every task with a due date. See when crunch time hits before it hits you.

Track everything
